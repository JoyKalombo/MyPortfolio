I am a Full Stack Software Engineer. I create websites for companies to have digital presence and be able to better impact the world with their services.

Learn about what I do

Here are the things I do.

There are three main aspects to a webpage I focus on.

Content of webpages

The content on a webpage is arguably the most important thing. It informs potential clients of your services.

Styling of webpages

The presentation of a webpage is important in capturing your audience. I work with you to design the webpage of your dreams to maximise engagement from clients.

Behavioural aspects of webpages

Some webpages require interactive behaviour. This can be necessary for webpages that enlarge images or pieces of text for the convenience of the user (as an example).

All of my work contains at least one of the three aspects mentioned.
